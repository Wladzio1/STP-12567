HOW TO PLAY

Manage, upgrade, expand

  • Start Production

    Collect milk and begin your initial operations.

  • Upgrade Equipment

    Improve machines to increase efficiency.

  • Automate Processes

    Unlock systems that generate income over time.

  • Expand Facilities

    Open new production areas and grow capacity

  • Figure out the money game

    Figure out when to upgrade and when to save up - it's trickier than it sounds!
