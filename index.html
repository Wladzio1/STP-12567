ABOUT THE GAME

Simple start, big growth

You'll start simple - just you, a cow, and some basic equipment - but before you know it, you're running a full operation.


Players manage resources, upgrade equipment, and unlock new stages of production.


The trick is knowing when to save up vs. when to splurge on upgrades - took me forever to figure that out!

HOW TO PLAY

Manage, upgrade, expand

  • Start Production

    Collect milk and begin your initial operations.

  • Upgrade Equipment

    Improve machines to increase efficiency.

  • Automate Processes

    Unlock systems that generate income over time.

  • Expand Facilities

    Open new production areas and grow capacity

  • Figure out the money game

    Figure out when to upgrade and when to save up - it's trickier than it sounds!
